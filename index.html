The Kingston Road Village Concert Series brings high-quality, entertaining concerts to the Upper Beach while supporting various fundraising activities. We present concerts that are exciting, challenging and diverse while bringing people together and spreading joy along the way. In an increasingly divided world, we believe that music is a necessary and positive force. Come join us for a fun ride! Tickets

Friday, May 29 @ 7:30 p.m.

Arkora

Composers Bach, Hildegard von Bingen, and DuFay find a meeting place in this concert of voices and instruments from different centuries and traditions, including alto sax, electric guitar, turntables, harmonium, strings, tabla, and analogue synths. Multi-instrumentalist Benton Roark and singer/conductor Kathleen Allan have conceived an immersive concert unlike anything you’ve heard before. See you there!
